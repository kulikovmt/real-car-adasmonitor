Detailed Description

Analyzes webcam frames to detect driver fatigue and distraction.

Uses a DNN-based face detector (Caffe SSD) and a Haar Cascade eye detector. Maintains a rolling history buffer of 30 frames to determine sustained eye closure (drowsiness alert: eyes closed in 20 of 30 frames). Head turn estimation is based on face center offset from frame center.
